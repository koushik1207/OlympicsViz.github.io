Continent Supremacy in the Olympics History

Keeping in sight the history of Olympics, we are trying to tell the history of our world in the last century. While most of the countries were still struggling with colonial rule or monarchies, handful of countries namely United States,Britain, France, Germany were dominating the Olympics. Continent Supremacy in the Olympics is evident from the above Visualization. Europe has always been dominant in the Olympics given it's size and number of countries in the European Continent. The second most dominant continent in the Olympics is America. The primary reason is USA's soveriegn in the Olympics.

Impact of World Wars on Olympics
